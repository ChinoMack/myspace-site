Valentino's Blurbs

About me:

I’m here to bring back the feeling of the early 2000s internet — when everything was new, creative, and a little chaotic in the best way.

This page is all about nostalgia core — music, YouTube, retro gaming, anime, and everything that made growing up online back then so memorable. If you remember customizing your page, watching low quality videos late at night, or discovering random things online, you’re in the right place.

I’ve always had a passion for video games since I was a kid. Some of my favorites on the PS2 were Dave Mirra, SSX, Tony Hawk, Def Jam: Fight for NY, and War of the Monsters.

Right now I’m working on a solo game development project called Lorenzo, inspired by that same era and everything that made those games feel special.

If you’re into the early 2000s vibe, feel free to check back in for updates — there’s more coming soon.

Thanks for stopping by my page. I appreciate you visiting, and I hope you come back again for more nostalgia.
